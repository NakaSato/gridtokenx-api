Admin calls POST /api/admin/meters/mint-from-reading
    ↓
Handler validates admin role & fetches meter reading
    ↓
Loads GRID_TOKEN_MINT from environment
    ↓
Derives user's Associated Token Account (ATA)
    ↓
Calls blockchain_service.mint_energy_tokens()
    ↓
Builds Anchor instruction:
  - Discriminator: SHA256("global:mint_tokens_direct")[0..8]
  - Data: amount as u64 little-endian
  - Accounts: [token_info_PDA, mint, user_ATA, authority, token_program]
    ↓
Signs & sends transaction to Solana
    ↓
Waits for confirmation
    ↓
Returns signature
    ↓
Updates meter_readings.minted = true
Stores mint_tx_signature in database
    ↓
Returns success with real blockchain signature
